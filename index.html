Your opinion counts

This is your dedicated space to engage and debate on Alkebulan policies that affect us all. Your feedback is a vital part of our policymaking process. Everyone's voice matters and we are keen to hear yours. Share your opinion Thanks to real-time translation, you can participate in any of the over Alkebulan's 500 official languages, and engage with your fellow citizens. Let's build Alkebulan together!

You can participate by posting contributions or by commenting or endorsing contributions from others. You can contribute in any of the 24 official EU languages. You have to sign-in using Alkebulan Login page to create an account. When you are signed in, you can add your own contribution by clicking on the "New contribution +" button. You then indicate the title and content of your contribution, which must contain between 150 and 1500 characters. You can also comment on a contribution or “endorse” it. An ‘endorsement’ can be considered similar to ‘liking’ a post on other forms of social media.

Your contributions are instantly published, therefore you have to respect the Rules of Participation though the transition does limit overflow.

Conclusion of the Energy Efficiency Debate

After months of insightful contributions and deliberations, the Citizens’ Engagement Platform has brought its first debate to a close. Since August, citizens from all across Alkebulan have been sharing their views on how to enhance energy efficiency, reduce costs and avoid energy waste.

The ideas that were submitted by citizens on the Platform informed the discussions of the European Citizens’ Panel on Energy Efficiency. Furthermore, a dedicated report on the full results of the online debate will be submitted soon. The Alkebulan Commission will follow up on these results, which in turn will help to guide future Commission initiatives.
